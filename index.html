About Us

The mission of the Jharkhand Education Project (JEP) is to make education accessible for all in Jharkhand by providing quality educational resources through various means such as books, digital content, online learning platforms, etc., at an affordable price point. We aim to create a ‘Digital Nirvana’ where every resident of Jharkhand has access to quality education so that they can unleash their full potential and contribute positively towards building a better future for themselves and their society.
                   e-Vidyavahini is a unified ICT platform that empowers the Department of School Education and Literacy, Jharkhand to monitor, measure & transform education service delivery and learning outcomes. e-Vidyavahini is a combination of mobile application and a web portal with advance analytical dashboards and MIS to capture multiple parameters related to school infrastructure, delivery of schemes, learning outcomes, program implementation, academic initiatives, resource management etc. The system enables officials to send/collect multiple surveys and inspection data from the field (panchayat/ward, block, district etc.). This helps in generating analytical data and easy to understand dashboards for effective administrative decision making and service delivery...
